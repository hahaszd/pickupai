FAQ

Common questions from tradies

Will customers know it's an AI?

The AI uses a natural Australian voice and sounds like a real receptionist. Most callers don't notice the difference. At the end of the call, the AI mentions it's an AI assistant — but by then, the job details are already captured and the customer is happy someone answered.

How long does setup take?

Under 10 minutes. You sign up, get a dedicated phone number within minutes, then set call forwarding on your mobile with one quick dial code. No apps to install, no hardware, no IT.

What if I want to answer the call myself?

The AI only picks up when you don't. Call forwarding is set to kick in after about 20 seconds of ringing. If you answer first, the AI doesn't activate. You're always in control.

Is there a lock-in contract?

No. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. The early bird rate ($149/mo) guarantees your price for 3 months, but you can still cancel at any time with no penalty.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

If you're happy, your early bird rate of $149/mo (inc. GST) starts automatically and is locked in for 3 months. After that, the standard rate is $199/mo. If not for you, cancel before day 14 and you won't be charged a cent. No awkward phone calls needed — just cancel from your account.

Can it handle emergencies properly?

Yes. The AI is trained to detect emergencies (burst pipes, gas leaks, sparking outlets) and flags them as high priority. You can also choose to have calls transferred to your mobile during business hours. After hours, the caller gets clear safety advice and a callback commitment.
