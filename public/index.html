FAQ

Common questions from tradies

Will customers know it's an AI?

The AI uses a natural Australian voice and sounds like a real receptionist. Most callers don't notice the difference. At the end of the call, the AI mentions it's an AI assistant — but by then, the lead is already captured and the customer is happy someone answered.

How long does setup take?

About 5 minutes. You sign up, we assign you a dedicated number, and you set call forwarding on your mobile (one quick dial code). No apps to install, no hardware, no IT.

What if I want to answer the call myself?

The AI only picks up when you don't. Call forwarding is set to kick in after about 20 seconds of ringing. If you answer first, the AI doesn't activate. You're always in control.

Is there a lock-in contract?

No. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. The founding offer ($149/mo) locks your rate for 3 months, but you can still cancel at any time with no penalty.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

If you're happy, your subscription starts automatically at $149/mo. If not, cancel before day 14 and you won't be charged a cent. No awkward phone calls needed — just cancel from your dashboard.

Can it handle emergencies properly?

Yes. The AI is trained to detect emergencies (burst pipes, gas leaks, sparking outlets) and flags them as high priority. During business hours, it can warm-transfer emergency calls directly to you. After hours, the caller gets clear safety advice and a callback commitment.
