# Small Business Expansion (Beyond Tradie)

This segmentation is designed for the current product shape: inbound-call heavy businesses that need fast lead capture and callback workflows, without deep scheduling or multi-system ticketing on day one.

## Tier A — Immediate Fit (start now)

These verticals can be piloted with minimal product change:

1. **Residential and commercial cleaning**
2. **Pest control**
3. **Appliance repair (in-home)**
4. **Removalists and small moving crews**
5. **Garage door and roller door servicing**
6. **Locksmith (dedicated vertical)**

Why now:

- missed-call cost is high
- inbound demand is phone-first
- capture + callback workflow is enough to deliver value

Validation actions:

- run 2-week outbound pilot per vertical (20-30 businesses)
- track: answered-call rate, captured-lead rate, callback SLA adherence
- ship one verticalized script variant per vertical

## Tier B — Near-Term Fit (needs light capability upgrades)

1. **Auto repair workshops**
2. **Beauty and hair salons**
3. **Pet grooming and pet care**
4. **Real estate agencies**
5. **Legal practices (small firms)**
6. **Accounting and tax practices**

Required upgrades:

- appointment-friendly intake fields
- compliance-safe language templates (especially legal/accounting)
- richer call-type branching (new appointment / reschedule / billing / follow-up)

Validation actions:

- run 5-10 design partner pilots per vertical
- test conversion with and without calendar integration
- define compliance checklist by vertical before full outbound

## Tier C — Later (product mismatch today)

1. **High-velocity restaurant reservations**
2. **Large e-commerce customer support**

Reason to defer:

- heavy real-time booking dependency or multi-channel service complexity
- current product is optimized for callback workflows, not full transactional support queues

## Vertical Prioritization Scorecard

Use this score (1-5 each):

- inbound call volume
- missed-call revenue impact
- urgency of response
- simplicity of intake
- compliance burden (reverse score)

Prioritize segments with total score >= 18.

## 90-Day Rollout Sequence

1. Month 1: cleaning + pest control + appliance repair
2. Month 2: movers + garage door + locksmith
3. Month 3: one Tier B pilot (auto repair) after appointment-flow improvements

## Success Metrics

- >= 5 Tier A verticals actively piloting
- >= 30% reduction in missed-call lead loss (self-reported + call logs)
- >= 60% of actionable inbound calls converted to callback tasks
- positive owner feedback on SMS summary readability and urgency labeling
